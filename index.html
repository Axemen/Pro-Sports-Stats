Professional Sports Salaries

A Look at MLB, NBA & NFL Salary Spend

States with the Highest Paid and Rated Athletes

Using the interactive map, look at the average success metric and salary for professional athletes from each state. This can be observed for the years 2011-2018. This data is based on what state the players were drafted from in each sport.

Spend and Performance...

This data shows relationships between team attendance or success compared to how much they are spending each year, broken down by sport. For attendance, we looked at the average attendance relative to stadium capacity for all three sports. Success for the different sports is defined by their win/loss record for that respective year.

Pick Three...

Use this feature to determine the best professional athlete that was drafted from each state. Here we identify the top player from each sport that has the highest success metric (in percentile) and also display what school they were drafted from.
